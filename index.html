About Me

The Dalmatian's is a small business runs by a middleschooler named Tara. We named it "Dalmatian's" because of the color brem that we sells. We sells two color brem which is black and white. A Dalmatian dog colors are black and white. Due to it's similarity, we named ourselves as The Dalmatian's. The Dalmatian's sells Brem. Which is a fermented rice cake. A Madiun's signature food. Madiun is a city located in East Java Province, Indonesia. It lies in the western part of East Java, near the border with Central Java. Here, we sell many and varios kinds of fermented rice cakes or brem. Brem is a traditional Indonesian fermented product made from glutinous rice. It is rich in carbohydrates, probiotics, and antioxidants. Brem can boost energy, support digestion, and improve appetite. Interested? Find out more below!
